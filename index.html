A Great Hockeist

Alexander Ovechkin is a famous Russian hockeist.



He was born in the seventeenth of september of nineteen eighty five. Alexander started his carrier when he was sixteen years old. Ovechkin had played in NHL, Washington Capitals, Dinamo and other hockey teams in his twenty year career. Alexander also took part in Olympic games.



He had won a Stanley cup, four bronze, two silver and three gold medals.
